ABOUT ASPIRE

Aspire, a techno management fest that we have hosted successfully in the premises of our esteemed institution in Eight consecutive years 2011 to 2019.Students from distinguished institutions across India took part in this techno management fest. Following the same tradition, we are organizing Aspire 2020 and hence providing a platform to explore the technological cosmos.

OUR MISSION

To mould youth into world class technocrats of tomorrow who would endeavor to increase the quality of life for human kind.

OUR VISION

To be an esteemed technical fest that can consistently evident that it keeps its strategic planning and in improving institutional research and effectiveness as a model of excellence and innovation in responsible areas.
